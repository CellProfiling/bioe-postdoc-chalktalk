BioE Postdoc Chalk Talk Series | BioE Beer, Boba, & Bites

Event Description

The BioE Postdoc Chalk Talk series aims to build community for BioE postdocs and to practice and gain familiarity with these important first few minutes of delivering an impactful chalk talk.

These events are followed by the BioE Beer, Boba, and Bites mixer to help give a place for BioE postdocs to gather and build connections.
